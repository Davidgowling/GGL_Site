About Gowling Group

Strategic investment and management for flooring and interiors brands.

Gowling Group Ltd is an investment and management business dedicated to building strong, sustainable brands within the flooring and interiors sector. We provide strategic direction, financial strength and shared operational capability that allow our group companies to grow with confidence.

Our activities span distribution, trade counters, product development and consumer brands — all underpinned by reliability, service and long-term partnerships with our customers and suppliers.
